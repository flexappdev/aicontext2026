# AI Future

Where AI is heading — near-term capabilities, likely breakthroughs, and the path to AGI.

---

## The 2026 Baseline

Before extrapolating, here's where we are:

| Capability | 2026 State |
|------------|------------|
| Coding | Autonomously solves complex GitHub issues (SWE-bench ~65%) |
| Context | 1M–10M token windows (entire codebases, libraries) |
| Reasoning | Extended thinking rivals PhD-level on many benchmarks |
| Agents | Multi-step tasks over hours — still fail on week-long projects |
| Vision | Photorealistic video generation (Veo 3, Sora) |
| Voice | Real-time conversation, voice cloning indistinguishable |
| Local | 70B models run on consumer GPUs |
| Cost | $0.10–$0.25/1M tokens for capable models |

## Near-Term (2026–2027)

### Coding & Software Engineering
- **AI writes most new code** — developers become spec-writers and reviewers
- SWE-bench approaches 80%+ — AI handles most GitHub issues autonomously
- **AI-maintained codebases** — agents fix bugs, refactor, and add features overnight
- Legacy code migration becomes an AI task (COBOL → modern stacks)

### Agents & Autonomy
- **Week-long task completion** — agents that book travel, manage projects, run research
- Multi-agent teams standard — orchestrator + specialist agents as the default architecture
- Computer use matures — agents operate full desktops, not just APIs
- **AI colleagues** — specialized agents with persistent memory and domain expertise

### Models
- 1T+ parameter MoE models with trillion-token context
- Real-time multimodal (see, hear, speak simultaneously)
- On-device frontier models (phone-class hardware runs GPT-4 equivalent)
- **Continuous learning** — models update from user interactions without full retraining

---

## Medium-Term (2027–2029)

### Scientific Research Acceleration
- **AI PhD-level research** in narrow domains (protein folding was just the start)
- Drug discovery timelines collapse from decades to years
- Materials science: AI discovers new battery chemistries, semiconductors
- Climate modeling improves significantly with AI-accelerated simulations

### Economy & Work
- **30–50% of white-collar tasks** automatable per McKinsey estimates
- New job categories: AI trainer, prompt engineer, agent orchestrator
- Companies run with 10x fewer employees in some sectors
- **AI-first startups** — 2 founders + 100 AI agents as the new playbook

### Robotics
- General-purpose robots become commercially viable (Figure, Boston Dynamics, Tesla Optimus)
- Warehouse automation reaches 80%+ penetration
- Physical AI (Embodied AI) emerges as next frontier after language

### Infrastructure
- **Custom silicon** — every major tech company has its own AI chip (Apple, Google TPU, AWS Trainium, Meta MTIA)
- Energy demand crisis — data center power = small countries
- Nuclear energy revival driven by AI data center demand

## Long-Term (2029–2035)

### Towards AGI
Different orgs define AGI differently:

| Definition | Threshold | Who Uses It |
|------------|-----------|-------------|
| "Can do most human jobs" | Economic productivity parity | OpenAI |
| "Recursive self-improvement" | AI improves AI faster than humans | MIRI |
| "Novel scientific discovery" | Solve open problems humans can't | DeepMind |
| "Pass any cognitive test" | Human-level on all benchmarks | Academic |

### Potential Trajectories

**Optimistic:** AI accelerates science, medicine, and education. Climate solved. Abundance for more people.

**Neutral:** Gradual disruption, regulatory frameworks emerge, productivity gains partially offset displacement.

**Risk scenarios:**
- Misalignment — AI pursues proxy goals that diverge from human values
- Concentration — AI capabilities controlled by a few companies/governments
- Acceleration — capabilities outpace our ability to understand or constrain them

## Emerging Research Areas

| Area | What It Is | Why It Matters |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| **Mechanistic Interpretability** | Understanding what's inside neural networks | Safety — know why models behave as they do |
| **Constitutional AI** | Training models to follow principles | Safer fine-tuning without human labeling |
| **Scalable Oversight** | Using AI to supervise AI | Can't manually check superhuman AI outputs |
| **Multiagent Coordination** | Multiple AI systems working together | Required for complex autonomous tasks |
| **World Models** | AI builds internal simulations of the world | Reasoning about physics, causality |
| **Embodied AI** | Robots that learn from interaction | Bridge between language and physical world |
| **Neurosymbolic AI** | Combining neural nets + logic/rules | Better reasoning, fewer hallucinations |

## Risks to Watch

### Technical Risks
- **Hallucination** — models confidently state false information
- **Jailbreaking** — adversarial prompts bypass safety training
- **Prompt injection** — malicious content hijacks agentic AI behavior
- **Model collapse** — training on AI-generated data degrades future models

### Societal Risks
- **Deepfakes** — photorealistic fake videos/audio at scale
- **Job displacement** — faster than retraining/safety nets can absorb
- **Concentration** — 3-5 companies controlling transformative technology
- **Surveillance** — authoritarian use of AI for control

### Existential Risks
- **Misalignment at scale** — superintelligent systems with subtly wrong goals
- **Bioweapons** — AI lowers barrier to designing dangerous pathogens
- **Cyberweapons** — AI-generated exploits outpace defenses

## What to Build For

If you're building in 2026, optimize for:

1. **Agents, not chatbots** — the shift from one-shot Q&A to multi-step autonomous tasks
2. **Memory + context** — personalized AI that remembers your projects and preferences
3. **Tool use** — AI that can call APIs, run code, browse the web, manage files
4. **Multimodal** — text + image + audio + video in single workflows
5. **Evaluation** — as AI generates more content, human judgment on AI output becomes the bottleneck
6. **Trust & verification** — users need to know what AI did and why
